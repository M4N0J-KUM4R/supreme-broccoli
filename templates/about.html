About CloudLab Terminal

Empowering developers with cloud-based learning and hands-on experience

Our Mission

CloudLab Terminal is dedicated to making cloud computing education accessible to everyone. We believe that hands-on experience is the best way to learn, which is why we've built a platform that gives you direct access to Google Cloud Shell and a full-featured IDE right in your browser.

Our goal is to remove the barriers to cloud learning by providing a seamless, integrated environment where you can learn, practice, and build real-world cloud applications without the hassle of complex setup or expensive infrastructure.

Key Features

Browser-Based Terminal

Access a full-featured terminal directly in your browser with xterm.js. No installation required.

Integrated Theia IDE

Edit code with a powerful cloud-based IDE that provides a VS Code-like experience.

Google Cloud Integration

Seamlessly connect to Google Cloud Shell with OAuth2 authentication for secure access.

Interactive Lab Guides

Follow step-by-step tutorials with integrated documentation and progress tracking.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your learning journey with detailed statistics and achievement milestones.

Structured Courses

Learn at your own pace with curated courses covering fundamentals to advanced topics.
